Support and speed up the software development process

Developing new programs and tools and updating the available software require time, and as we know, time is precious. If you appreciate my work or if you want to support or speed up the the software development process, you can donate or buy me gifts (which are related to my work).

All the software products are designed to have further improvements, and I also have in mind to develop many other free and useful software. But because I have a lots of other activities to do besides programming, it usually takes time to improve the released programs or to launch new ones. But you can change that...

If you use any of my software products and you need further improvements, or you simply appreciate my work and also you would like to see new improvements or programs appearing in the future, then you can indicate me to concentrate more on the software development to the detriment of other activities I do - by donating or buying me software/hardware equipment.

You can use any of the options aside to make a contribution and the result will be a speed up in the software development process. Also, don't forget to tell me what you would like to see improved, changed or new in the available software or on this website.

Bitcoin (BTC) is an online digital currency that is based on an open-source, peer-to-peer encryption protocol, and is not managed by any government or central authority.
